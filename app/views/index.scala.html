    About this application

    Golf Handicap is a FREE online handicap and statistics tracker to help you make the most out of your golf game

    Our Application gives you the ability to enter your handicap, and will automatically track and update your handicap as you play your rounds. We will also record the statistics for your rounds, and give you a real look at the state of your game!

    Requirements to play Golf Handicap

    All you need to make the most out of this application is to have a valid handicap issued by the GUI. However if you don't, we will give you the option to start at the maximum allowable handicap!
